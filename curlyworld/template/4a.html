4A Curl Type

Overview

This is the first form of type 4 curls. These curls are known for their well defined loopy 'S' curls. Unfortuantly, this hair is prone to frizzing and loosing its definition. Circumference of the hair can be compared to that of a crochet needle and it typically has less natural protection from damage.

Hairstyles

Prime hairstyles for 4a hair are wash and go's, braid outs, twist outs, and protective styling.
Other hairstyle methods such as flexi-rods and bantu knots are typically a little harder to achieve because
of the thickness and the coarseness of the hair. If you attempt these styles then they'll most likely contain frizz
or the defintion won't hold as well.

Protective Hair Styles

Protective hairstyles that do not include false hair:
Halo Braids
Bantu Knots
Cornrows

Protective Hairstyles that do include false hair:
Havana Twists

Protective styles can help your hair growth increase and gives you a slight break on maintaining your natural hair.
