3B Curl Type

Overview

This hair type has stretched spiral curls, that elongate towards the shoulders.

Products

Cantu
SheaMoisture
Mixed Chicks

Hairstyles

This hair type is best styled in wash and go's, flexi rods, twist outs, and braid outs.

Protective Hair Styles

This hair is more corse than 3A hair, but it is still not thick enought for false hair protective styles. Flat twist and cornrows are recommended.
