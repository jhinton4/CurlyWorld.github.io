4B Curl Type

Overview

This is the second form of type 4 curls. These curls are known for their well defined 'Z' curls. These curls dont have a specific curl pattern because the hair bends in sharp angles
like the letter 'Z'. Type 4b is tightly coiled and can feel wiry to the touch and it can range from thin to wiry and coarse.
The shrinkage level on type 4b is typically up to 75% of its actual hair length.
